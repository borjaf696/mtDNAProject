Full Length (FL) sequences are now classified into three subsets: aDNA for ancient DNA, CancerCL for cancer, tumor, other abnormal tissues, or cell line studies, and Main for the rest.

The current sequence counts are from two sets of human mitochondrial sequences collected from GenBank on Sep 01, 2020. These sets consist of:

  • 51,673 Full Length (FL) sequences (>15.4 kb)
    • FL.Main: 48,606
    • FL.aDNA: 1,305
    • FL.CancerCL: 1,762 (582 cancer + 1,180 cell lines)
  • 74,660 Control Region (CR) only sequences (0.4-1.6 kb)
